  • Visualization is a very useful technique for stress reduction.
  • Start by taking deep breaths in through your nose, exhaling through your mouth. Focus on deep breathing, filling your belly with air. Adjust the pulse tempo to match your breath if you find it too fast or slow. You can also move the cursor to learn how the nebula webs react.
  • When you are ready, adjust your breathing and fixate the cursor at the center of the screen. Watch as the webs starts to collopse into a circle. Imagine that this circle is your stress.
  • Watch as it slowly builds and accumulates on top of previous stress. Notice how tense the circle becomes. After a few moments, swipe the cursor off the canvas. Watch as the circle dissipates away and imagine it as your stress.
  • Repeat this as many times as you like and take any liberties you wish. When you are ready, hit the play button on the top left-hand corner of the screen.
