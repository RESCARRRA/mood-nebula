Welcome to the Visualzation Meditation Page.

Visuapzation is a very useful technique for stress reduction.

Start by moving the cursor and learning how the nebula webs react.

Once you are done, fixate the cursor at the center of the screen.

Watch as the webs starts to collopse into a circle.

Imagine that this circle is your stress.

Watch as it slowly builds and accumulates on top of previous stress.

Notice how tense the circle becomes.

After a few moments, swipe the cursor off the canvas.

Watch as the circle dissipates away and imagine it as your stress.

Repeat this as many times as you pke and take any pberties you wish.

When you are ready, hit the play button on the top left-hand corner of the screen.

Use headphones for best results.
